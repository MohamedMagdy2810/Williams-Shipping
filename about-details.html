About Williams Shipping

Your trusted shipping agency and logistics service provider in all Egyptian ports

Company Overview

Williams Shipping Agencies S.A.E was established as an Egyptian stock company to act as a shipping agency and logistics service provider in all Egyptian ports for all types of vessels.

Behind this success is a strong belief about customer service and doing business with integrity and abiding by business ethics.

Information technology has become an essential tool not only as a knowledge base but also vital for providing high level of customers service that our agents and customers expect. In this respect, we have invested heavily in IT either in hardware or software to ensure the best service and meet customers' needs. We have developed our own in-house logistics and shipping software that ensures efficient and effective control of our services. We have also spent a lot of time and effort to upgrade our ERP systems to be in line with the pace of technology.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

Our MISSION is to extend and enhance our services to our principals and customers, by providing high quality differentiated and innovative shipping and logistics services. We strive to grow and add value to our customers' businesses. This is attained by providing comprehensive set of services, continuously investing in state-of-art technology and developing our staff to fully address our principals and customers' needs.

Our Vision

Our VISION is to be amongst the top regional innovators in the field of shipping agency and logistics services providers in order to maximize the value added to our principals as well as customers.

Strategic Locations

Williams Shipping is perfectly positioned in this industry throughout a strong presence in all Mediterranean and Red Sea ports of Egypt specially: Alexandria, El-Dekheila, Abu Qir, Damietta, Port Said, Adabiya, Sokhna, and Safaga ports.

We handle diversified types of vessels such as, dry bulk carriers, tankers, general cargo and RORO vessels.

As cited by our major clients, this remarkable milestone has been achieved due to our reliable and competent performance and an outstanding capability in managing the Ship - Port Interface daily challenges encountered in this global industry.

Our Services

Our services and activities include:

Vessel Operations

  • Timely definite reporting to our principals for all operational minutes relevant to vessels' attendance
  • Arranging all port formalities in due time
  • Updating all concerned parties with vessels' status/prospects
  • Attendance on board by our port staff
  • Sending all related documents to all concerned parties including NOR and SOF

Protecting Agency

We act as a protecting agent for our principals when other agents are appointed by counterparts, having been entrusted with this role we entirely cover vessels' status during port stay, reporting relevant prospects and events to our principals while also carrying out all relevant matters that we may be assigned of.

Disbursement Accounts

Provide port disbursement estimates for calling vessels to be settled under final port disbursement in accordance with original official invoices and vouchers received from port authorities and concerned parties.

Owner's Services

  • Crew Change / Crew Sign in – sign off – visits
  • Medical assistance to the crew
  • Cash to Master
  • Garbage/sludge/bilge/slop disposal
  • Forwarding of Documents
  • Bunker and provision supply and bonded stores supply
  • Repair and Maintenance
  • Customs clearance and delivery for import shipments to vessel

Suez Canal Transit

Williams Shipping is also well positioned in providing Suez Canal transiting services. Through our dedicated team in both ports of Port Said and Suez we ensure smooth and cost effective transit services for ship owners who entrust us in this service.

We are experienced in arranging transit of large units like floating docks which need special treatment to ensure smooth/fast transit.

Additional Services

  • Appointing and supervising stevedoring operation
  • Appointing and supervising warehousing operation
  • Safety and marine pollution prevention
  • Parcels' delivery
  • Online vessels status, useful market information on the web
  • Periodic ports and market reports to our principals
