Our Story

Your Trusted Shipping Partner Since 2005

Who We Are

Williams Shipping Agencies S.A.E was established as an Egyptian stock company to act as a shipping agency and logistics service provider in all Egyptian ports for all types of vessels.

Since our inception, we have grown from a small local shipping agency to a comprehensive logistics provider with a presence across major Egyptian ports. Our journey has been marked by continuous growth, innovation, and a steadfast commitment to excellence.

Our Technology

Information technology has become an essential tool not only as a knowledge base but also vital for providing a high level of customer service that our agents and customers expect. In this respect, we have invested heavily in IT, both in hardware and software, to ensure the best service and meet customers' needs.

We have developed our own in-house logistics and shipping software that ensures efficient and effective control of our services. We have also spent a lot of time and effort to upgrade our ERP systems to be in line with the pace of technology.

Our Team

Our success is built on the expertise and dedication of our team. We employ professionals with extensive experience in shipping, logistics, and international trade. Our staff undergoes regular training to stay updated with the latest industry trends and best practices.

We believe in fostering a collaborative work environment where innovation and creativity are encouraged. Our team's diverse backgrounds and skills enable us to provide comprehensive solutions to our clients' complex shipping and logistics challenges.

Our Future

As we look to the future, we remain committed to our mission of providing high-quality, differentiated, and innovative shipping and logistics services. We will continue to invest in technology, expand our network, and develop our team to better serve our clients.

We are excited about the opportunities ahead and look forward to growing our partnerships with clients and stakeholders in the shipping and logistics industry.
